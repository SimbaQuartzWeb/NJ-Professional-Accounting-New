Start-Ups

We all know that most startups struggle to raise enough capital to stay in business. As a result, NJ Professional Accounting provides very affordable packages for entrepreneurs. Our Chartered Professional Accountant provides you with high-level accounting and tax compliance services without the need to invest in a full-fledged accounting department.

Small and Medium Businesses

To streamline their operations, small businesses require proper accounting and bookkeeping. Keeping your books in order allows you to understand the challenges your company is facing and make changes that promote growth. To meet your needs, NJ Professional Accounting provides a wide range of accounting and taxation services.

Self-Employed

Being self-employed and managing your own business requires a significant amount of effort and commitment. N.J Accounting will handle your personal taxes, HST returns, and will assist you in keeping accurate financial records. N.J Accounting has also invested in various accounting software and online management tools.

Real Estate

Investing in real estate presents numerous challenges, and you must be prepared to ensure that your money is well invested. N.J Professional Accounting provides real estate investors, builders, and contractors with a variety of tax services. We can analyze key financial ratios and prepare you for any loan application in addition to preparing tax returns and financial statements.
