Tax-Preparation & Planning

In Canada, we prepare personal (T1), corporate (T2), and trust (T3) tax returns. We have vast experience in the field of income tax preparation, and our expertise is backed by our strong integrity and client relationships.

corporate and Personal  tax returns are becoming more complicated as a result of the Canada Revenue Agency's constant changes and amendments to the tax rules. This can be difficult for individuals and business owners to keep up with while running their businesses. Allow us to handle this for you while you focus on growing your business and working.



We strive to reduce your tax liabilities by focusing on both domestic and international taxation. We also provide various compliance services and represent you before taxing authorities. Our fully automated system ensures that all of your documents are securely stored electronically.

We provide services for:


Personal tax preparation and tax planning

Unincorporated businesses, partnerships, rental properties, professionals, capital gains and losses, investment accounting etc.

Scientific Research and Experimental Development Tax Credits.

Accounting and taxation for Professionals including doctors, dentists, chiropractors, social workers, engineers, architects etc.

Dispute resolution with the tax department including representation on your behalf.

Past due tax returns and amended tax returns
